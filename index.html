About Us

Transforming ICT in Nigeria

THE GUIDANCE ICT LTD is a leading provider of ICT training, equipment, and solutions, serving universities, corporate clients, and government sectors across Nigeria. With a focus on innovation and reliability, we partner with global tech brands and financial institutions to deliver top-tier services.

Our Mission

To empower institutions and organizations with advanced ICT solutions and training for sustainable growth.

Our Vision

To be the leading ICT solutions provider in Nigeria, bridging the gap between technology and education.

Our Reach

Operating across states like Osun, Ondo, Ogun, Ekiti, Enugu, Oyo, Kwara, Borno, Kaduna, and Kano.
